About Us

Overview of Activities

For over 10 years, we have been helping to develop ventures and improve relationships between government and private sectors in conventional and renewable energy business and operations. Our experience has given us unique capabilities to combine commercial and technical know-how towards understanding complex challenges and problems throughout the energy markets of the MENA region.  

Our Mission

Creating impactful investments through extensive experience in developing energy projects and ventures

Improve uncertainty

Manage opportunities and risks through unique knowledge and analytics.

Align stakeholders

Mobilize resources

Optimize returns

Optimize returns of new and existing ventures through combined commercial industry and technology expertise, along with strong GCC and MENA presence.
